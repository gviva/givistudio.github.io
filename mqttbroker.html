Privacy Policy for Light Broker

This application runs locally on your device.

Data Collection

The app does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal user data to external servers. All MQTT messages are processed in RAM and discarded or logged locally on the screen only.

Permissions

  • Network: Used solely to accept local TCP connections.
  • Foreground Service: Used to keep the broker running when the app is minimized.
  • Wake Lock/Battery: Used to prevent the OS from closing active network connections.

Logs

Logs displayed in the app are temporary and deleted when the app is closed.
