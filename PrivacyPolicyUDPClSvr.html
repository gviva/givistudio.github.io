Privacy Policy for Maui UDP App

This application runs locally on your device.

Data Collection

The app does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal user data to external servers. All UDP messages are processed in RAM and discarded or logged locally on the screen only.

Permissions

Internet: Used solely to send and receive local UDP packets. The app does not connect to the internet unless you explicitly target a public IP address.
Network/WiFi State: Used to check if the device is connected to a network before attempting to send data.

Logs

Logs displayed in the app are temporary and deleted when the app is closed. No history is saved to persistent storage.
