Privacy Policy for MeshTalk

Effective Date: May 1, 2026

MeshTalk is a local network walkie-talkie and communication application. Your privacy is our priority. This application operates entirely on your local network (LAN/Wi-Fi).

Data Collection & Privacy

MeshTalk does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal user data, audio recordings, or chat messages to any external servers, third-party analytics, or cloud services. All communications (audio and text) are transmitted directly peer-to-peer over your local network using UDP protocols and are immediately discarded from memory once played or when the app is closed.

Required Permissions and Why We Need Them

Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO): Required to capture your voice when you press the Push-To-Talk (PTT) button. The audio is streamed locally to selected devices on your network and is never saved.
Foreground Service & Wake Locks: Required to keep the app listening for incoming walkie-talkie calls and network messages even when the app is running in the background or the screen is turned off.
Network & Wi-Fi State: Used to discover other MeshTalk users on the same Wi-Fi network and to send/receive local multicast packets.

Data Retention

We do not retain any data. Chat histories and network logs are stored temporarily in your device's RAM and are permanently deleted as soon as the application is closed.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
